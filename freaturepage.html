Join BookShare Today

Share Books With Friends & Community

BookShare makes it easy to swap books with other readers in your area or across the country.

  • Find readers with books you want and ones who want yours
  • Simple request system to arrange book exchanges
  • Direct messaging to coordinate meetups or shipping
  • Track your shared and received books in one place
  • Build a trusted network of fellow book lovers

Rate & Review Your Reads

Keep track of what you've read and share your thoughts with the community.

  • Rate books on a five-star scale
  • Write detailed reviews or quick reactions
  • See what your friends think about books you're interested in
  • Get personalized recommendations based on your ratings
  • Discover new books through community reviews

Track Your Reading Journey

Keep detailed reading logs to monitor your progress and celebrate your literary achievements.

  • Log start and finish dates for each book
  • Set and track annual reading goals
  • View beautiful statistics about your reading habits
  • Keep notes and favorite quotes as you read
  • Look back on your reading history with pride
